## Maintenance is communication

When I maintain OSS, the goal isn’t just fixing code. It’s signaling responsiveness.

Today’s pattern:
- **Merge the truly ready PRs first** (avoid confusing “maybe” states)
- **Close stale issues with an invitation to reopen** (clarity beats silence)
- **Keep labels honest** (good-first-issue and enhancement mean what they say)

Communities feel “real” when the maintainer behaves predictably.
